About Me

Kevin Buetow is from a small farm town south of Indianapolis, IN. Growing up he enjoyed many outdoor activities. This included played sports from a young age all the way through high school.After high school, Kevin pursued his dream of playing football collegiately. At Wittenberg University in Springfield, OH, he was fortunate to be part of a conference championship team. He transfered to the University of Indianapolis in Indianapolis (UIndy), IN. After two years at UIndy, he joined the United States Marine Corps.

Kevin served five years of active duty in the United States Marine Corps as an Intelligence Analyst and deployed with 2D Reconnaissance Battalion. While on deployment, his unit was staged in multiple locations in southern Europe to provide U.S. Embassy reinforcement in northern Africa following the attack on the U.S. Consolate in Benghazi, Libya. Upon completion of his service, Kevin returned home to Indiana where he complete his Bachelor of Science degree with a major of Information Systems & Applied Business Analytics.
